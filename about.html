Stockholm Leather Social Calendar About

Stockholm Leather Social is an initiative to bring leather subculture into the streets. We want to facilitate safe and social spaces and encourage local community in a public setting.

Presently we organize a monthly bar meet for old friends and new.

Our ambitions are regular and diverse socials. Get in touch if you'd like to be involved.

FAQ

Is it men only?
No. All genders welcome.
Is there a dresscode? / Must I wear leather?
No, but gearing up is always appreciated.
Is there an entry fee?
No.
